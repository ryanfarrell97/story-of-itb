About the College

The college itself currently has 8 buildings, with 1 of these due to be completed in 2019.
The 7 completed buildings are the A block, the LINC building, the C block, the D block, the E block, the F block and the S block. The 8th building is the new student services that will open sometime in 2019.
The A Block, the first building built, is home to the NLN offices.
The LINC Building is home to many different companies and contains one of the main Mac computer rooms
The C Block is the central hub of the college. It contains the SU office, a common room for students, health and support services, the sports hall and the canteen.
The D block contains the photography studio, another Mac lab and a huge lecture theatre.
The E Block is a standard building with lecture theatres in it.
The F Block contains the Mac computer room with the most Macs in it, the college reception, library and the SID.
The S Block contains the gym and another big lecture theatre.
